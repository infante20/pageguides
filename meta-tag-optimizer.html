Page Title
This is the description that will appear in search results. Ensure it is relevant, natural, and contains a compelling call to action.

Optimization Pro Tips

  • The ideal title length is between 50 and 60 characters to prevent truncation by Google.
  • Include your primary keyword as close to the beginning of the title as possible.
  • The meta description should act as a quick "sales pitch" to convince users to click (CTR).
  • Avoid "keyword stuffing"; write for humans and optimize for algorithms.
