Frequently Asked Questions

What is Schema Markup and why do I need it?

Schema markup is structured data that helps search engines understand your content better. Using our Schema Generator can help you earn "Rich Snippets" like star ratings or FAQ accordions, which significantly improve your Click-Through Rate (CTR).

How do I fix duplicate content issues?

The most effective way is by using a canonical tag. Our Canonical Tag Generator creates the code you need to tell Google which version of a page is the "official" one, consolidating your equity and ranking power.

Are these tools updated for 2026?

Yes. All generators, including our Meta Tag Optimizer and Open Graph Tool, follow the latest requirements from Google, Facebook (Meta), and LinkedIn to ensure your site stays ahead of the competition.
