Recommended Alt Tag

SEO & Accessibility Tips:

  • Keep it concise (usually under 125 characters).
  • Don't start with "Image of..." or "Graphic of...".
  • Avoid "keyword stuffing" - be natural.
  • If it's decorative, use an empty alt attribute: alt="".
