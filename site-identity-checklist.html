Site Identity Checklist

A strategic audit for brand consistency and E-E-A-T signals.

✓

Logo Transparency

Favicon and OG images should have consistent, high-quality transparent versions.

✓

Color Palette Consistency

Brand colors are reflected in meta tags (theme-color) and CSS variables.

✓

Social Media Handles

Usernames are consistent across X, LinkedIn, and Instagram for easier discovery.

✓

Author Byline & Bio

Blog posts include author names and bios to verify expertise (EEAT).

✓

Contact Information

Physical address or verified email is present in the footer or contact page.

✓

Schema Branding

Organization schema includes Logo URL and SameAs social links.
